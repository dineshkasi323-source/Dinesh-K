# Kids Mountain Trip Video Prompt
## 1-Minute Video Concept

**Title:** "First Mountain Adventure"

---

## Scene Breakdown & Timing Guide

### **Opening (0:00–0:06) — 6 seconds**
- **Scene:** School bus pulling up at a forested mountain trailhead
- **Visuals:** Wide shot, warm golden-hour sunlight, gentle handheld camera movement
- **Camera:** Establishing wide shot, slight pan to kids exiting bus
- **Dialogue:** Kid 1 (excited, bright tone): "We made it — mountains!"
- **Audio:** 
  - Music: Upbeat, playful acoustic guitar with light percussion (fades in)
  - SFX: Bus door creak, children chatter, excited voices
  - Ambient: Gentle wind rustles

---

### **Preparation (0:06–0:18) — 12 seconds**
- **Scene:** Kids putting on backpacks, lacing shoes, adjusting hats
- **Visuals:** Close-ups of kids' faces (ages 6–10), colorful jackets and backpacks
- **Camera:** Quick 1–2 second cuts with subtle push-ins; medium shots showing details
- **Dialogue:** None (or light background chatter)
- **Audio:**
  - Music: Continues upbeat tempo, subtle wind rustle layered
  - SFX: Zipper sounds, Velcro straps, shoe tugs, backpack clicks
  - Ambient: Birds chirping in distance

---

### **Trail Adventure (0:18–0:36) — 18 seconds**
- **Scene A (0:18–0:26):** Walking up gentle trail; two-shot of best friends skipping over roots, one child helping another across a log
  - **Camera:** Follows at knee height, smooth steadycam/gimbal work
  - **Dialogue (voice-over, playful):** "Race you to the top!" with laughter
  - **Audio:** Guitar picks up tempo, light xylophone melody layered on top
  - **SFX:** Footsteps on leaves, twigs cracking, joyful laughter

- **Scene B (0:26–0:36):** Discovery moment — kids find a small stream, examine colorful pebbles
  - **Camera:** Slow 3-second macro close-up of pebble in sunlight, shallow depth of field
  - **Dialogue (whisper, gentle):** "Look at this shiny one!"
  - **Audio:** Music softens to gentle guitar pluck, water trickle SFX amplified
  - **SFX:** Water flowing, subtle splashes, excited whispers

---

### **Triumph (0:36–0:52) — 16 seconds**
- **Scene:** Kids reaching a scenic lookout with valley view in background
- **Visuals:** Golden backlight, wide establishing shot, joyful expressions
- **Camera:** Quick drone-like pullback (or simulated pullback effect); wide aerial perspective
- **Dialogue (group moment):** Group cheer, one child shouts "We did it!"
- **Audio:**
  - Music: Swells into bright, triumphant chorus with handclaps and tambourine
  - SFX: Wind whoosh, faint echo of cheers, celebratory sounds
  - Ambient: Mountain breeze, distant valley sounds

---

### **Montage & Finale (0:52–1:00) — 8 seconds**
- **Scene:** Candid moments — high-fives, snack sharing, pointing at distant peak, thumbs-up to camera
- **Visuals:** Quick cuts between moments; final close-up of smiling child
- **Camera:** Warm close-up on last shot, slight vignette effect; freeze-frame or gentle crossfade
- **Dialogue:** Child looks directly at camera and says, "Best trip ever!"
- **Audio:**
  - Music: Last musical chord resolves, soft applause SFX
  - Title card appears with text: "First Mountain Adventure — Memories Made" with date and simple animated leaf graphic
  - SFX: Gentle fade-out sound

---

## Audio Mix Details

### **Music Specifications:**
- **Genre:** Upbeat, playful acoustic/indie
- **Instruments:** Acoustic guitar (primary), light xylophone, soft percussion, handclaps, tambourine
- **Tempo:** Moderate-fast, energetic but not frantic (around 120 BPM)
- **Arc:** Starts gentle, builds through middle, peaks at triumph moment, gentle resolution
- **Mood:** Adventurous, wholesome, inspiring

### **Sound Effects (SFX):**
- Bus door creak
- Children chatter and laughter
- Zippers and Velcro
- Shoe tugs and clicks
- Footsteps on leaves and gravel
- Twigs cracking
- Water flowing and splashing
- Wind rustles and whoosh
- Celebratory cheers and claps
- Distant bird chirps

### **Dialogue Delivery:**
- All children (ages 6–10)
- Natural, energetic (not overly staged or scripted-sounding)
- Clear enunciation
- Short, punchy lines
- Excited but authentic tone

---

## Visual Style Notes

- **Color Palette:** Warm, saturated greens and golds; sunny, bright highlights
- **Lighting:** Golden-hour sunlight, natural outdoor lighting, slight lens flare okay
- **Tone:** Upbeat, adventurous, wholesome, family-friendly
- **Pacing:** 
  - Quick cuts (1–3 seconds per shot) for energy and movement
  - Longer holds (3–5 seconds) for emotional close-ups and discoveries
  - Medium shots (2–3 seconds) for action sequences
- **Camera Movement:** Mix of handheld smoothness, steadycam, and subtle aerial perspectives
- **Overall Feel:** Real, authentic childhood adventure; feel-good and inspiring
